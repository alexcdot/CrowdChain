Our Mission

Road traffic is a huge problem in LA and cities all across the world. It decreases productivity and steals otherwise fun or useful time. We aim to solve this problem through the use of blockchain. Crowdchain leverages the power of blockchain to create distributed, enforceable contracts and the ability of large groups of people to make change happen. Click the pink button to the right and join our fight to destroy traffic once and for all.

We espouse an all or nothing methodology. After you sign up, there is no committment. However, when participation hits 80% of LA residents, our smart contract kicks in. You will receive an award for participating in a huge shift to public transportation along with the vast majority of our city's population. The roads will be for the first time, clear.

Scan your phone at QR codes available on buses and subways. This allows us to make sure that you're holding up your end of the deal. In return, we will make the cryptocurrency flow. Thanks again for participating, and we hope you join our movement.
